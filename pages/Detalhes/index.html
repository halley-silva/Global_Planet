Technical Data

Average distance from the Sun: 228 million km.

Day length (Sun): 24 hours and 37 minutes (very close to Earth).

Gravity: 3.71 m/s² (approximately 38% of Earth's gravity).

Average Temperature: -62°C (ranging from -125°C in winter to 20°C in summer).

Records and Superlatives

Olympus Mons: The largest volcano in the Solar System.

Valles Marineris: A canyon system that, if it were on Earth, would cross the USA.

Luas: Possui dois satélites naturais pequenos e irregulares, Fobos e Deimos.
